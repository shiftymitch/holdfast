Services Offered

Production Manager

Production Managers are responsible for overseeing the production of events, including festivals, concerts, and other live events. They work with the event promoter, venue, artist, tour, and vendors to ensure that the event runs smoothly and meets the client's expectations.

Tour Manager

Tour Managers are responsible for coordinating all aspects of a concert tour, including travel, accommodations, and scheduling. They work closely with the venue, promoter, artist, band, and crew to ensure that the tour runs smoothly and that everyone is well taken care of.

Site Director

Site Directors are responsible for overseeing the setup and operation of event sites, including festivals, concerts, and other live events. They work with the production team, vendors, and venue staff to ensure that the site is safe, secure, and well-organized.

Stage Manager

Stage Managers are responsible for coordinating all aspects of the stage, including setup, sound, lighting, and scheduling. They work closely with the production team, artists, and crew to ensure that the stage runs smoothly and that the show is a success.

Quartermaster

Quartermasters are responsible for managing the inventory of equipment and supplies for events, including festivals, concerts, and other live events. They work with the production team, vendors, and venue staff to ensure that all necessary equipment and supplies are on hand and in good working order.

Festival Director

Festival Directors are responsible for overseeing the planning and execution of festivals, including lineup, production, and logistics. They work with the event promoter, production team, and vendors to ensure that the festival is a success and meets the client's expectations.

Site Ops

Site Ops are responsible for overseeing the operations of event sites, including setup, security, and logistics. They work with the production team, vendors, and venue staff to ensure that the site is safe, secure, and well-organized.

Site Design

Site Designers are responsible for designing the layout and setup of event sites, including festivals, concerts, and other live events. They work with the production team, vendors, and venue staff to create a site that is safe, secure, and well-organized.

Artist Advancing & Logistics

Responsible for coordinating travel, accommodations, and scheduling for an artist on tour. They work closely with artist & management to ensure the event has been properly advanced and all logistics are taken care of ahead of time.

Equipment Rentals

Equipment Rentals are responsible for providing the necessary equipment and supplies for events, including festivals, concerts, and other live events. They work with the production team, vendors, and venue staff to ensure that all necessary equipment and supplies are on hand and in good working order.

Staffing

Staffing are responsible for providing the necessary staff for events, including festivals, concerts, and other live events. They work with the production team, vendors, and venue staff to ensure that all necessary staff are on hand and ready to work.

Production Rentals

Production Rentals are responsible for providing the necessary production equipment and supplies for events, including festivals, concerts, and other live events. They work with the production team, vendors, and venue staff to ensure that all necessary production equipment and supplies are on hand and in good working order.

Production Design

Production Designers are responsible for designing the production elements of events, including festivals, concerts, and other live events. They work with the production team, vendors, and venue staff to create a production that is visually appealing and enhances the overall event experience.

Branding & Sponsorship Activations

Responsible for creating and executing branding and sponsorship opportunities for events, including festivals, concerts, and other live events. They work with the event promoter, sponsors, and vendors to create unique and engaging activations that enhance the event experience and drive brand awareness.

About Us

Driven by a desire to exceed expectations and create memorable experiences, Brendan approaches every project with enthusiasm and dedication. His commitment to excellence, coupled with his passion for innovation, sets him apart in an industry known for its dynamism and competitiveness.
